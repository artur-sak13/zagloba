Projects & Programs

Development of Polish Youth Cultural Institutions

  • Establishing new "Polish Clubs" at Universities with Polish communities
  • Developing and cultivating pre-existing Polish clubs and providing resources to connect with other schools and the greater Polish community
  • Reaching out to officially established Polish schools (K-12) and helping older students prepare to transition to University life

Creation of Cultural Programs and Philanthropy

  • Volunteering in Polish events and/or organizations throughout the Chicagoland area
  • Creating and offering scholarships for Polish students heading to college
  • Leadership/Polish Youth Conference

Networking

  • Connecting with the Polish professional community
  • Providing opportunities for young Polish entrepreneurs to grow their businesses

Zagłoba's Involvement

A brief history

  • Officially announced in the Summer of 2016 that we will be joining the Polish Student Leaders Assembly
  • Developed proposals to organize and structure the organization more effectively
  • Reached out to Loyola, DePaul, UIC, Northwestern, Univeristy of Chicago, and Lake Forest College to organize branches of PSLA within each respective school
  • Recently expanded from leaders within executive positions to all determined members within Zagłoba and other Polish clubs
