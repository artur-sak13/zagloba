About Us

Zagloba Polish Club is a cultural and professional organization aimed at UIUC students who are native polish speakers, individuals of Polish heritage, and those interested in learning Polish. Zagloba Polish Club sponsors various social events including barndances, semi-formals, Polish folk-dancing, coffee hours, and other social and philanthropic events.
